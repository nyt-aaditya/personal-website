Movies

  • 3 Idiots


    "3 Idiots" is a 2009 Indian comedy-drama film directed by Rajkumar Hirani. The story follows three engineering students—Rancho, Farhan, and Raju—at a prestigious college in India. Through their journey, the film explores the pressure of the education system, the importance of following one’s passion, and the value of friendship. With humor, emotion, and a powerful message, "3 Idiots" encourages thinking differently and living life on your own terms. The film stars Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, and Sharman Joshi, and is widely praised for its storytelling and social commentary.

  • Wake Up Sid


    "Wake Up Sid" is a 2009 Indian coming-of-age film directed by Ayan Mukerji. It tells the story of Sid Mehra, a carefree and spoiled college student in Mumbai, who learns about responsibility and self-discovery after meeting Aisha, an aspiring writer from Kolkata. As Sid begins to grow and find his passion for photography, the film beautifully captures the transition from adolescence to adulthood. Starring Ranbir Kapoor and Konkona Sen Sharma, "Wake Up Sid" is a heartfelt tale about finding purpose and growing up.
